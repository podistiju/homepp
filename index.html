Professional Postpartum Care at Home

Comprehensive midwifery and lactation support for new families in the Tri-Cities area as well as Pitt Meadows and Maple Ridge. MSP covered services with home visits for your comfort and convenience.

Our Services

Full Postpartum Care

  • 6 weeks of postpartum care covered by MSP
  • No healthcare provider referral needed - self referral accepted
  • Self refer during pregnancy or up to 13 days after birth
  • All visits are home visits throughout the 6 weeks
  • Comprehensive postpartum health monitoring
  • Newborn care and feeding support

IBCLC Lactation Consulting

  • MSP covered up to 6 weeks (healthcare provider referral required)
  • Service available beyond 6 weeks postpartum (private pay)
  • Self referral accepted for private pay services
  • Breastfeeding support and education
  • Latch assessment and improvement
  • Pumping and milk supply guidance
